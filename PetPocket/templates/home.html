How long does shipping take for my pet's goodies?
We strive to deliver your pet's goodies within 3-5 business days across most regions. For rural areas, it might take up to 7 days. You'll receive a tracking link as soon as your order ships, so you can keep an eye on its journey!
Are your products safe for my pet's sensitive tummy?
Absolutely! We carefully source products with natural ingredients, and many are vet-approved for pets with sensitive stomachs. Check the product details for specific dietary info, or reach out to us for personalized recommendations.

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy! If your pet isn't thrilled with a toy, you can return it in its original condition for a full refund or exchange. Just contact us, and we'll guide you through the process.
